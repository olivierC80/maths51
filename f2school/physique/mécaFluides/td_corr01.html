$$ p_D - p_C = \rho_{mercure} g (Z_C - Z_D) $$

$$ p_D = p_C + d \rho_{eau} g (-h) $$

$$ p_B - p_D = \rho_{eau} g (Z_D - Z_B) $$ $$ p_B = p_D + \rho_{eau} g y $$

$$ p_B = p_A + \rho_{eau} g x + h \rho_{eau} g (1-d) + \rho_{eau} g y $$ $$ p_B - p_A = \rho_{eau} g (x+y) + h \rho_{eau} g (1-d) $$
